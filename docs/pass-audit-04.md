# Pass 4 Audit — Vertical and Heavy Civil Sublogic

- Added schema support for:
  - equipment records,
  - material records,
  - historical estimate intelligence.
- Seeded heavy-civil equipment and material records.
- Expanded Operations page to show:

  - production,

### Heavy civil
Now more directly represented:

### Vertical
Now more explicitly grouped as a technical workflow zone with RFIs, submittals, meetings, and document-control intent.

### Historical intelligence
Seeded examples for historical bid / unit-cost / production-rate memory.

## Audit verdict
This pass strengthens the non-simple operating modes substantially and gives the platform more authentic construction-specific depth.
